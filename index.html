About Me

Dedicated to Aviation Safety

I am Gyan Ranjan, a Senior Officer in Aircraft Rescue and Firefighting (ARFF) at Mumbai International Airport Limited (CSMIA/MIAL) — one of India's busiest airports.

With a strong academic foundation from the National Fire Service College (NFSC), Nagpur — a premier institution under the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India — I bring technical expertise in fire engineering and a deep commitment to aviation safety.

Recognized as a Young Safety Professional (Fire) 2025, I specialize in ICAO compliance, Safety Management Systems, and emergency response operations. My work directly impacts the safety of thousands of passengers and crew members daily.
