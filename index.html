About Me

Dedicated to Aviation Safety

Gyan Ranjan is currently working as Senior Officer – ARFF at Mumbai International Airport Limited (CSMIA). He holds a B.Tech in Fire Engineering from NFSC, Nagpur, along with an Advanced Diploma in Industrial Safety from the National Institute of Labour Education and Management, Chennai.

He has successfully completed IFE Level 3, UK (Grade – I) Certification from the UK and a Postgraduate Qualification in Disaster Management. He has also completed multiple Aviation-related courses authorized by ICAO and DGCA, along with NEBOSH IGC (IGC-1 and IGC-2) in occupational safety.

As a professional Trainer with over 5 years of experience, he has delivered Fire Safety Training, conducted Mock Drills, and provided Practical Demonstrations. He has also served as a Guest Faculty (Fire & Safety) at VPM's Polytechnic, Thane for Fire Engineering students.

He has undergone practical training attachments with Delhi Fire Service and Karnataka Fire Service, attending more than 250 fire calls across Delhi, Bengaluru, and Mangaluru, plus a 10-day specialised training at UL Laboratory, Bengaluru covering foam testing, fire forensics, NFPA 921 investigation, and fire dynamics simulation.

He is passionate about Innovative Solutions to Modern Fire Safety Challenges with a strong focus on R&D, technical paper writing, and Evacuation Modelling. During his graduation he published three research papers in international journals covering flame behaviour dynamics, flame-retardant materials, and firefighter PPE selection.
